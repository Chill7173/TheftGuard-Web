System Guide

Your complete guide to monitoring your grid and detecting power theft.

Quick Start Guide
1. Theft Detection

The system compares the Source (grid power) with the Load (household consumption). If Source > Load by more than 0.2A, a Critical Red Alert triggers instantly, indicating potential theft or bypass.

2. Dashboard Analytics

The Dashboard shows real-time charts: Blue chart = House Load, Yellow chart = Grid Source. Compare them to detect anomalies.

3. Cost Tracking

The Cost tab calculates your monthly bill based on consumption and rate per unit. Set your local electricity rate to get accurate estimates.

4. Rules & Automation

Use the Rules tab to create automated responses (alerts, emails, relay disconnect) when thresholds are exceeded or anomalies detected.

5. Advanced Analytics

The Analytics tab shows usage patterns, peak times, efficiency scores, and anomaly heatmaps over time periods.

6. Dataset & Filtering

The Dataset tab allows you to load, filter, and analyze raw data. Export summaries as CSV for further analysis.

7. Hardware Control

The Control tab lets you remotely toggle the relay to disconnect power. Use with caution in a safe environment.

8. Email Reports

Enable email notifications in Settings to receive daily/weekly reports, alerts, and summaries of your energy consumption.
